For education & entertainment only. SimiWine makes an educated guess from a wine's appearance, structure and aroma. The grape, region and age it shows are approximate ranges, not identifications, and can be thrown off by grape variety, region, oak, winemaking style and storage — a heat-damaged young wine can mimic an old one. Always confirm against the actual label. This is not professional or commercial advice. Please enjoy wine responsibly, in moderation, and only if you are of legal drinking age.
